Lasagna Recipe

Lasagna is a tasty delicacy from Italy!

Nothing better came from the country of the big shoe.

The Lasagna has the following ingredients:

  • Tomato sauce
  • Cheese
  • Salt
  • Noodle plates
  • Vegetables

But the right order to use them is:

  1. Lay your Noodle plates on your baking plate.
  2. Then put the tomato sauce and the cut vegetables on that.
  3. Salt can be placed with pepper.
  4. Repeat that as often as you like your lasagna in aspect of height.
  5. Now put the baking plate in the oven and bake that with 200 degrees Celsius for twenty minutes.
  6. ENJOY!
